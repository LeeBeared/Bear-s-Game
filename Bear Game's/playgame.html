Rules Below

Click Deal button to deal a hand of five cards.The first two cards will be filped over to show the value. Click one of the remaning cards to filp over it over. To stand, click the Stand button this will tell your hand value. Clicking the stand but will also show you your point for that round. To restart the game, click the Reset button. Restarting the game will set your points bank back to 1000.

  • 30: 200 points
  • 28 or 29: 150 points
  • 26 or 27: 100 points
  • 24 or 25: 50 points
  • 22 or 23: 25 points
  • Under 22: -25 points
